## Welcome

This is **ide9x.dev** — my corner of the internet for security research,
bug bounty writeups, and notes on the things I break.

To write a new post, just drop a markdown file in `content/posts/` with
frontmatter like this:

```md
---
title: "My SSRF writeup"
date: "2026-06-15"
tags: ["ssrf", "writeup"]
excerpt: "Short summary shown on the homepage."
---

Your content here...
```

The post appears automatically on the homepage, newest first.

### What to expect here

- Vulnerability writeups
- Bug bounty notes
- Tooling and methodology

> Recon is not a weekend activity — it's a lifestyle.

Stay tuned.
